Accessibility

One thing that drives me is equity, diversity, and inclusion. As part of that there are many accessibility changes that can be consider during web design and implementation!

I will be incorporating these as I go and keeping track of useful resources here for others to use too.

Key Accessibility Tips

  • Use alternative text tags effectively with good descriptions, not just "image of x"
  • Check the colour contrast of websites is still readable for those with visual impairments. How it looks to you may be different for others!
  • Use appropriate and well structured headers, footers, main content. This makes it easier to navigate with accessibility tools
